Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Gymr handles your data:

  • Information Collection: We collect your email for account creation (via Supabase) and your workout data to calculate leaderboard rankings.
  • Location Data: We access your location only to find nearby gyms and verify your presence for territory claims. We do not track you in the background.
  • Data Usage: Your points and username are displayed publicly on gym leaderboards to facilitate competition.
  • Data Deletion: You can request full account and data deletion at any time by emailing us at the support address above.
